Visit to a Weird Planet
by
  • Jean Lorrah
  • Willard F. Hunt

Description A story (a 'real person fic', in part) in which Kirk, Spock, and McCoy are somehow accidentally beamed to the (then-) present-day set of Star Trek, filming in progress, and must pretend to be their counterparts (Shatner, Nimoy, and Kelley) until they can be rescued.
Notes

Great literature it's not, but it's a serviceable bit of comedy. This story would have a number of sequels by different authors.

Published in Spockanalia #3.
