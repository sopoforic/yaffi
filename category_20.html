alternate universe

Some change has occurred before the story begins.

Usually, this means something substantial about the setup is different. "Usagi woke up five minutes earlier in the first episode and missed meeting Luna" doesn't count. "Usagi was never born" probably does. "The Moon Kingdom is alive and well and ruling the Earth in the modern day" definitely does.
