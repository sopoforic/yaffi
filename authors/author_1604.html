The Territory of Rigel
by
  • Dorothy Jones

Description A song written by Dorothy Jones. She introduces it as "a piece supposed to have been written by Spock, many years ago." She describes it as a "Vulcan form called ni var" which "means literally 'two form' . . . a piece comparing and contrasting two different things or two aspects of the same thing."
Notes

It's an interesting exercise, but not to my taste as poetry.

Published in Spockanalia #1.
